What We Do

We Take Care

Welcoming infants and toddlers for their initial dental check-ups to monitor dental development and provide guidance for parents.

Preventive Care

Offering dental cleanings, fluoride treatments, dental sealants, and routine oral exams to prevent cavities and promote optimal oral health.

Dental Education

Educating children and parents about proper oral hygiene habits, nutrition, and the importance of regular dental visits.

Treatment of Dental Issues

Diagnosing and treating a wide range of dental issues using child-friendly techniques and materials.

Orthodontic Evaluation

Providing early orthodontic evaluations to monitor dental development and identify potential bite issues or alignment problems.

Behavior Management

Using gentle behavior management techniques to help anxious or fearful children feel comfortable during their dental appointments.

Special Needs Dentistry

Providing compassionate dental care for children with special needs, ensuring they receive the attention and support they deserve.

Dental Tourism

Pediatric Dental Tourism In India

India is one of the leading players in the dental tourism/healthcare facilitation industry. It is increasingly emerging as the destination of choice for a wide range of dental procedures. There are numerous advantages of coming to India for treatment. Some of the advantages are: Internationally accredited dental facilities using the latest technologies. Highly qualified pediatric Dentists / Anaesthesiologists / Maxillofacial surgeons and hospital support staff. Significant cost savings compared to domestic dental healthcare. Dental treatment costs in India are lower by at least 60-80% when compared to similar procedures in USA and the UK. No Wait Lists. Fluent English speaking staff. Can easily be combined with a holiday / business trip.

Thousands of people every year fly from Middle East, USA, and Europe to tour places in India for a grand holiday and dental care. The cost of dental care in the western countries is approximately 10 times more than that in India. Dental tourism in India offers several packages. However, these will be based on the requirements of the person and their holiday needs.
