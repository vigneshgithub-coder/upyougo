Our Philosophy

An exclusive child friendly dental clinical setup with an optimistic ambience focusing on preventive dentistry following the concept of "Dental Home" for Newborns, Infants, Toddlers, Teens and Special Children. An exclusive Dental Clinic for Children Aged 0 -18 years in the city of Health(CARE), Coimbatore. What is Dental Home - " It's an ongoing relationship between the dentist and the patient, inclusive of all aspects of oral health care delivered in a comprehensive, continuously accessible, coordinated, and family-centered way."

Don’t be discouraged if your child cries on the first visit to the dentist. Crying is a normal reaction for some children when faced with a new situation. Remember to compliment your child to others no matter how your child performed during their visit. it is important they feel reassured and positive about their dental experience. The more positive we all make the visit the more they will want to come back and try even harder next time! If you get home and realize there was something you forgot to ask, don’t hesitate to call our office. We realize the first appointment can be overwhelming and we want you to feel you have had your questions answered completely. We strive to make each and every visit to our office a fun one!
